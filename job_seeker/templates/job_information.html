Introduction

This position is for a skilled Software Engineer to join our dynamic team in Gauteng. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality software applications. We are looking for a proactive individual with a passion for technology and a drive to deliver innovative solutions.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Experience with data structures, algorithms, and software design patterns.
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  • Experience with version control systems like Git.

Experience

  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in software development or a related field.
  • Experience with software testing and debugging.
  • Experience in building and maintaining software systems.
  • Knowledge of software development life cycle (SDLC) and agile methodologies is preferred.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain software applications and systems.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and implement new features.
  • Write clean, scalable, and efficient code while adhering to best practices.
  • Debug and troubleshoot software issues, providing timely resolutions.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to the improvement of development processes.
  • Keep up to date with the latest industry trends and technologies to ensure the software remains current and competitive.
